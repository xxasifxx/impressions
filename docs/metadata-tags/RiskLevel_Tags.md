**Strategy:** Ensure appropriate service matching based on risk tolerance and safety considerations

## Risk Assessment and Management

### Pre-Service Risk Evaluation:
**Client Health History:**
- Allergies and sensitivities
- Previous adverse reactions
- Current medications
- Skin conditions
- Pregnancy status

**Service-Specific Assessments:**
- **Chemical Services:** Patch testing 24-48 hours prior
- **Heat Services:** Heat sensitivity evaluation
- **Mechanical Services:** Skin integrity assessment
- **Near-Eye Services:** Eye health and contact lens considerations

### Risk Mitigation Strategies:

#### Minimal-Risk Services:
- **Standard Consultation:** Basic health and preference discussion
- **Basic Preparation:** Clean tools, fresh products
- **Gentle Techniques:** Conservative approach
- **Easy Reversal:** Simple correction methods available

#### Moderate-Risk Services:
- **Enhanced Consultation:** Detailed health history
- **Patch Testing:** When chemicals are involved
- **Professional Assessment:** Experienced staff only
- **Monitoring:** Check client comfort throughout service

#### Higher-Risk Services:
- **Comprehensive Consultation:** Extensive health and beauty history
- **Mandatory Patch Testing:** 24-48 hours prior to service
- **Expert-Only Services:** Senior staff or specialists
- **Detailed Aftercare:** Comprehensive post-service instructions

#### Extreme-Risk Services:
- **Medical Clearance:** May require doctor approval
- **Multiple Consultations:** Planning sessions before service
- **Master-Level Expertise:** Only most experienced professionals
- **Comprehensive Aftercare:** Detailed healing and maintenance protocols

## Risk-Based Bundling Considerations

### Low-Risk Combination Bundles:
- **Safe Starter Package:** Haircut + Eyebrow Threading + Basic Facial
- **Gentle Maintenance:** Blow Out + Eyebrow Tinting + Scalp Massage
- **Risk-Averse Client:** Consultation + Patch Test + Minimal Service

### Mixed-Risk Bundles:
- **Graduated Risk:** Start with minimal-risk, add moderate services
- **Balanced Approach:** One higher-risk service with minimal-risk supports
- **Safety-First:** Extensive consultation before any moderate+ risk services

### High-Risk Exclusive Packages:
- **Expert-Only Services:** Only for experienced, risk-comfortable clients
- **Comprehensive Preparation:** Multiple consultations and patch tests
- **Premium Support:** Enhanced aftercare and follow-up

## Emergency Protocols by Risk Level

### Minimal-Risk Emergencies:
- **Minor Irritation:** Immediate service cessation, gentle cleansing
- **Client Discomfort:** Adjustment of technique or products
- **Basic First Aid:** Standard comfort measures

### Moderate-Risk Emergencies:
- **Allergic Reactions:** Immediate product removal, antihistamine consideration
- **Chemical Burns:** Immediate neutralization and cooling
- **Professional Consultation:** Contact with senior staff

### Higher-Risk Emergencies:
- **Severe Reactions:** Immediate medical evaluation consideration
- **Chemical Injuries:** Emergency protocols and medical referral
- **Documentation:** Detailed incident reporting

### Extreme-Risk Emergencies:
- **Medical Emergencies:** Immediate medical attention
- **Serious Complications:** Emergency room referral
- **Legal Documentation:** Comprehensive incident documentation
