## Cross-Enhancement Bundling Opportunities

### Complete Facial Enhancement Package:
- **Base:** Premium Facial + Eyebrow Laminating + Lash Extensions
- **Add-On:** Formal Makeup + Lip Treatment
- **Occasion:** Bridal or special event preparation

### Hair & Face Harmony Bundle:
- **Base:** Full Highlights + Eyebrow Tinting + Facial
- **Logic:** Color coordination between hair and brows, skin prep for new look
- **Target:** Clients wanting complete transformation

### Maintenance & Enhancement Combo:
- **Base:** Root Touch-Up + Eyebrow Threading + Express Facial
- **Logic:** Regular upkeep services that complement each other
- **Target:** Busy professionals maintaining their look

### Special Occasion Preparation:
- **Base:** Hairstyle + Bridal Makeup + Lash Extensions + Full Face Threading
- **Logic:** Complete preparation for important events
- **Target:** Brides, prom, formal events

## Business Benefits

### 1. **Natural Cross-Selling**
- Services grouped by enhancement goals create logical upsells
- Clients see value in completing their desired look
- Higher average order values through complementary services

### 2. **Goal-Oriented Marketing**
- Market packages based on desired outcomes
- Clear value proposition for each enhancement focus
- Targeted promotions for specific beauty goals

### 3. **Client Journey Mapping**
- Track client preferences across enhancement categories
- Develop loyalty programs based on enhancement focus
- Personalized service recommendations

### 4. **Staff Specialization**
- Stylists can specialize in specific enhancement areas
- Cross-training opportunities in related services
- Expert positioning for complex enhancement goals

**This enhancement focus tagging system creates natural service groupings that align with client beauty goals, enabling intuitive cross-selling and comprehensive beauty experiences.**
