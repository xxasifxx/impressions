## Scheduling Strategy Recommendations

### Peak Hour Optimization
- **Quick Services (≤30 min):** Fill gaps between longer appointments, lunch hour bookings
- **Standard Services (30-90 min):** Main appointment slots during business hours
- **Extended Sessions (90+ min):** Early morning starts, dedicated transformation days

### Staff Allocation Guidelines
- **Junior Staff:** Quick services and standard maintenance treatments
- **Senior Staff:** Extended sessions and complex procedures requiring expertise
- **Specialists:** Advanced techniques like microblading, color correction, bridal services

### Client Communication Framework
- **Clear Time Expectations:** Provide realistic duration estimates for each service category
- **Preparation Instructions:** Category-specific pre-appointment guidance
- **Scheduling Flexibility:** Multiple options to accommodate different time commitments
