## Scheduling Strategy

### Peak Hour Optimization:
- **Quick Services:** Fill gaps between longer appointments
- **Standard Services:** Main appointment slots during business hours
- **Extended Sessions:** Early morning starts or dedicated days

### Staff Allocation:
- **Junior Staff:** Quick services and standard maintenance
- **Senior Staff:** Extended sessions and complex procedures
- **Specialists:** Advanced techniques requiring expertise

### Client Communication:
- **Clear Time Expectations:** Realistic duration estimates
- **Preparation Instructions:** What to expect for each time category
- **Scheduling Flexibility:** Options for different time commitments

---

## Business Benefits

### 1. **Appointment Efficiency**
- Better schedule utilization with varied service lengths
- Reduced waiting times and gaps
- Improved staff productivity

### 2. **Client Satisfaction**
- Realistic time expectations prevent disappointment
- Options for different schedule constraints
- Better work-life balance accommodation

### 3. **Revenue Optimization**
- Quick services for impulse bookings
- Extended sessions for premium pricing
- Bundle opportunities within time constraints

### 4. **Operational Planning**
- Staff scheduling based on service complexity
- Equipment and space allocation
- Inventory planning for different service types

---

**This time investment tagging system enables efficient scheduling, realistic client expectations, and optimal resource utilization while creating natural bundling opportunities within time constraints.**
