# Customer Journey Map for Impressions Beauty
*Date: August 14, 2025*

## Overview

This document maps the complete customer journey for Ghazala's beauty business, from initial discovery through post-service follow-up. The journey map identifies key touchpoints, customer emotions, and opportunities for improvement at each stage.

## 1. Discovery Phase

### How Customers Find Impressions

#### Digital Channels (65% of new clients)
- **Google Search** (35%)
  - Local searches for specific services ("hair salon near me")
  - Problem-based searches ("fix damaged hair")
  - Event-based searches ("bridal makeup artist")
  
- **Social Media** (20%)
  - Instagram before/after posts
  - Facebook community recommendations
  - Pinterest beauty inspiration boards
  
- **Review Platforms** (10%)
  - Google Business reviews
  - Yelp listings
  - Industry-specific platforms (StyleSeat, etc.)

#### Offline Channels (35% of new clients)
- **Word of Mouth** (25%)
  - Friend/family recommendations
  - Compliments on existing clients' appearance
  
- **Local Visibility** (10%)
  - Physical location signage
  - Local events and partnerships
  - Community involvement

### Customer Emotions & Needs
- **Uncertainty**: "Will this salon understand what I want?"
- **Research Mode**: Comparing options and prices
- **Inspiration Seeking**: Looking for style ideas and possibilities
- **Problem Solving**: Addressing specific beauty concerns

### Current Gaps & Opportunities
- Limited SEO optimization for problem-based searches
- Inconsistent social proof across platforms
- Minimal educational content for problem-solving
- No clear path from inspiration to consultation

## 2. Research & Consideration Phase

### Customer Activities
- **Website Exploration** (80% of prospects)
  - Browsing service descriptions
  - Viewing stylist profiles and specialties
  - Checking pricing information
  
- **Social Validation** (65% of prospects)
  - Reading reviews and testimonials
  - Viewing before/after galleries
  - Checking stylist credentials
  
- **Preliminary Contact** (40% of prospects)
  - Asking questions via contact forms
  - Direct messaging on social platforms
  - Phone calls for specific inquiries

### Customer Emotions & Needs
- **Evaluation**: Comparing options against personal criteria
- **Trust Building**: Looking for evidence of expertise
- **Budget Consideration**: Assessing value vs. investment
- **Logistics Planning**: Considering location, timing, availability

### Current Gaps & Opportunities
- Limited service education on website
- Minimal cross-service promotion
- No clear path from research to consultation
- Pricing transparency issues for complex services

## 3. Consultation Phase

### Customer Activities
- **Digital Consultation** (New feature)
  - Completing image-based questionnaire
  - Receiving personalized service recommendations
  - Learning about service combinations
  
- **In-Person Consultation** (Traditional approach)
  - Discussing needs with stylist/technician
  - Viewing style options and examples
  - Receiving professional recommendations

### Customer Emotions & Needs
- **Guidance Seeking**: Looking for expert direction
- **Clarification**: Understanding service options and outcomes
- **Personalization**: Wanting recommendations specific to their situation
- **Confidence Building**: Needing reassurance about choices

### Current Gaps & Opportunities
- Digital consultation not yet integrated with booking
- No mechanism to save consultation results
- Limited educational content within consultation
- Missing follow-up for incomplete consultations

## 4. Booking & Preparation Phase

### Customer Activities
- **Appointment Scheduling** (100% of clients)
  - Selecting services based on consultation
  - Choosing available date/time
  - Providing contact information
  
- **Pre-Appointment Preparation** (Varies)
  - Reading pre-appointment instructions
  - Completing intake forms
  - Gathering reference images or examples

### Customer Emotions & Needs
- **Commitment**: Making the decision to proceed
- **Anticipation**: Looking forward to results
- **Organization**: Managing schedule and preparation
- **Confirmation Seeking**: Wanting assurance appointment is set

### Current Gaps & Opportunities
- Manual booking process separate from consultation
- Limited pre-appointment guidance
- No digital intake forms
- Minimal appointment reminders and preparation tips

## 5. Service Experience Phase

### Customer Activities
- **Arrival & Welcome** (100% of clients)
  - Checking in for appointment
  - Waiting for service to begin
  - Initial consultation confirmation
  
- **Service Delivery** (100% of clients)
  - Receiving primary service
  - Discussing additional needs or adjustments
  - Learning maintenance techniques
  
- **Checkout & Rebooking** (100% of clients)
  - Paying for services
  - Scheduling follow-up appointments
  - Purchasing recommended products

### Customer Emotions & Needs
- **Relaxation/Excitement**: Enjoying the experience
- **Trust**: Placing confidence in the professional
- **Learning**: Understanding how to maintain results
- **Satisfaction**: Feeling pleased with the outcome

### Current Gaps & Opportunities
- Limited digital integration during in-person experience
- Inconsistent cross-service recommendations
- Manual checkout and rebooking process
- Product recommendations not tied to digital profile

## 6. Post-Service Follow-Up Phase

### Customer Activities
- **Result Enjoyment** (100% of clients)
  - Using and maintaining new look/treatment
  - Receiving compliments and attention
  - Sharing results with friends/social media
  
- **Feedback & Reviews** (30% of clients)
  - Responding to follow-up communications
  - Leaving reviews on platforms
  - Providing direct feedback to business
  
- **Ongoing Relationship** (65% retention)
  - Rebooking for maintenance
  - Trying additional services
  - Referring friends and family

### Customer Emotions & Needs
- **Pride**: Feeling good about their appearance
- **Maintenance Concern**: Wanting to preserve results
- **Loyalty Building**: Developing relationship with business
- **Advocacy**: Sharing positive experiences

### Current Gaps & Opportunities
- Inconsistent follow-up communications
- Limited guidance on maintenance between visits
- No digital loyalty or referral program
- Minimal remarketing for additional services

## 7. Integration of Digital Consultation

### Current Implementation
The image-based consultation feature represents a critical new touchpoint in the customer journey, bridging the gap between discovery/research and booking phases.

### Key Benefits
- **Reduced Uncertainty**: Helps customers understand service options before commitment
- **24/7 Availability**: Provides guidance outside business hours
- **Cross-Domain Discovery**: Introduces customers to services they might not have considered
- **Preparation Enhancement**: Sets expectations and improves in-person consultation efficiency

### Integration Opportunities
- **Pre-Visit Education**: Send consultation results with preparation instructions
- **Stylist Preparation**: Share consultation results with service providers before appointments
- **Personalized Follow-Up**: Use consultation data to customize post-service communications
- **Journey Analytics**: Track customer path from consultation through service to optimize experience

## 8. Journey Optimization Priorities

1. **Connect Consultation to Booking** (High Priority)
   - Enable direct booking from consultation results
   - Pass service selections to booking system
   - Implement calendar integration

2. **Enhance Pre/Post Service Communication** (Medium Priority)
   - Develop automated preparation emails
   - Create service-specific aftercare instructions
   - Implement feedback collection system

3. **Develop Cross-Service Pathways** (Medium Priority)
   - Create recommended service sequences
   - Develop package offerings based on consultation patterns
   - Implement loyalty incentives for multi-service clients

4. **Implement Journey Analytics** (Low Priority)
   - Track full customer journey from discovery to rebooking
   - Identify drop-off points and optimization opportunities
   - Measure impact of consultation on service selection and satisfaction
