### 🎯 **Agent Coordination Protocol**

#### **Phase 1: Individual Tagging (Parallel)**
- Each agent works independently on their assigned services
- Apply all 13 metadata categories to each service
- Follow the exact format specified in `METADATA_TAGGING_GUIDE.md`
- Complete all assigned services before coordination

#### **Phase 2: Cross-Validation (Collaborative)**
- Review tag consistency across similar services
- Validate ServiceCompatibility_Tags for cross-category bundling
- Ensure logical coherence in pricing and complexity alignment
- Coordinate on edge cases and borderline classifications

#### **Phase 3: Quality Assurance (Systematic)**
- Verify all 85 services have complete tag coverage
- Test tag logic through sample filtering scenarios
- Validate business logic alignment with stakeholder expectations
- Prepare for engine replacement deployment

### ✅ **Success Metrics**
- **Coverage**: 85 services × 13 categories = 1,105 tag assignments
- **Consistency**: Logical alignment across related services
- **Completeness**: No missing tags or categories
- **Quality**: Tags enable intelligent filtering and bundling
