## Usage Guidelines

### **For Client Matching:**
- Use primary emotional need as starting filter
- Consider secondary emotional contexts for refinement
- Match service intensity to client's emotional readiness
- Align service approach with desired emotional outcome

### **For Service Recommendations:**
- **Stressed clients:** Focus on relaxation-focused + confidence-building
- **Special events:** Prioritize celebration-ready + transformation-oriented
- **Self-improvement:** Emphasize transformation-oriented + confidence-building
- **Maintenance:** Highlight confidence-building services

### **For Marketing & Communication:**
- Emphasize emotional benefits in service descriptions
- Use emotional context language in client communications
- Create service packages based on emotional journey
- Train staff to recognize and respond to emotional cues

**These mappings enable precise emotional context filtering while maintaining the flexibility of multi-tag service categorization.**
