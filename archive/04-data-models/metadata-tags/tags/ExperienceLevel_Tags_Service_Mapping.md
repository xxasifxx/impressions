## Experience Level Progression Paths

### Natural Client Journey Examples:

**Hair Color Journey:**
1. **Beginner:** Hair Gloss → Hair Consultation
2. **Intermediate:** Toner → Partial Highlights → Hair Color
3. **Advanced:** Full Highlights → Balayage → Color Correction

**Eyebrow & Lash Journey:**
1. **Beginner:** Eyebrow Tinting → Lash Tinting → Eyebrow Shaping
2. **Intermediate:** Eyebrow Laminating → Lash Lift → Eyelash Extension
3. **Advanced:** Microblading → Lash Volume Extensions

**Facial Treatment Journey:**
1. **Beginner:** Express Facial → Hydrating Facial → Men's/Sensitive Skin Treatment
2. **Intermediate:** Diamond Glow → Anti-Aging Treatments → LED Light Therapy
3. **Advanced:** Chemical Peel

**Makeup Service Journey:**

2. **Intermediate:** Dinner Party → Formal Party → Bridal Silver/Gold

## Business Applications

### 1. **Client Onboarding**
- New clients start with beginner-friendly services
- Build trust and comfort before advancing to complex treatments
- Reduce service anxiety and improve satisfaction

### 2. **Staff Assignment**
- Junior stylists handle beginner-friendly services
- Senior staff focus on advanced-expertise services
- Optimize skill utilization and training progression

### 3. **Marketing & Recommendations**
- Target beginners with gentle, low-commitment services
- Upsell intermediate clients to advanced treatments
- Create service packages that follow natural progression

### 4. **Risk Management**
- Minimize complications by matching client experience to service complexity
- Ensure proper consultation for advanced services
- Reduce liability through appropriate service matching

---

**This comprehensive mapping enables the tag-based filtering system to match clients with appropriate services based on their experience level, creating natural progression paths and improving overall client satisfaction and safety.**
