## 💡 **Usage Guidelines**

### **For Client Safety:**
- Always check eligibility requirements before service
- Document any health conditions or restrictions
- Obtain necessary medical clearances
- Maintain detailed client health records

### **For Risk Management:**
- Use eligibility tags to identify potential risks
- Ensure proper insurance coverage for services
- Train staff on eligibility assessment
- Maintain safety protocols for all eligibility categories

### **For Service Customization:**
- Adapt services to meet eligibility requirements
- Modify techniques for specific conditions
- Select appropriate products for client needs
- Adjust service delivery for individual requirements

### **For Legal Compliance:**
- Follow age-related service restrictions
- Comply with health and safety regulations
- Maintain proper documentation
- Ensure informed consent for all services

### **For Client Communication:**
- Clearly explain eligibility requirements
- Discuss any restrictions or modifications needed
- Educate clients on safety considerations
- Set appropriate expectations based on eligibility

**These eligibility tags ensure safe, appropriate service delivery while maintaining clear categorization for business operations.**
