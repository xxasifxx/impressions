# Experience Level Tags

**Purpose:** Filter services by user comfort and skill level  
**Strategy:** Ensure natural progression paths from beginner to advanced services

---

## Experience Level Progression Paths

### Beginner → Intermediate Progression:
- **Hair:** Basic Cut → Hairstyle → Partial Highlights
- **Eyebrows:** Eyebrow Tinting → Eyebrow Laminating → Advanced Shaping
- **Facial:** Basic Facial → Anti-Aging Facial → Premium Treatment
- **Makeup:** Dinner Party → Formal Party → Special Occasion

### Intermediate → Advanced Progression:
- **Hair:** Partial Highlights → Full Highlights → Color Correction
- **Lashes:** Lash Tinting → Lash Lift → Lash Extensions
- **Waxing:** Basic Areas → Full Body → Brazilian
- **Makeup:** Formal Party → Bridal Trial → Bridal Day

### Cross-Domain Experience Building:
- **Beauty Newcomer:** Basic Cut + Eyebrow Tinting + Simple Facial
- **Regular Client:** Styling + Eyebrow Laminating + Anti-Aging Facial
- **Beauty Enthusiast:** Full Color + Lash Extensions + Premium Facial + Bridal Makeup

---

## Business Benefits

### 1. **Natural Client Journey**
- Clear progression from simple to complex services
- Builds client confidence and trust over time
- Reduces intimidation factor for new clients

### 2. **Staff Training Alignment**
- Junior staff handle beginner-friendly services
- Senior staff focus on advanced expertise services
- Clear skill requirements for each service level

### 3. **Risk Management**
- Beginners start with low-risk services
- Advanced services reserved for experienced clients
- Reduces service complications and dissatisfaction

### 4. **Revenue Growth**
- Natural upselling path as clients gain experience
- Higher-value advanced services for loyal clients
- Retention through progressive service offerings

---

**This experience level tagging system creates natural progression paths that guide clients from basic services to premium offerings while ensuring appropriate service matching and risk management.**
