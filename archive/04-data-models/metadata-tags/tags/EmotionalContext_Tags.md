## 💡 **Usage Guidelines**

### **For Service Recommendations:**
- Match service energy to client's emotional state
- Consider desired emotional outcome in service selection
- Adapt service delivery to emotional support needs
- Align service approach with comfort level

### **For Client Communication:**
- Use emotional context to guide conversation tone
- Acknowledge client's current emotional state
- Focus on desired emotional outcomes
- Provide appropriate level of emotional support

### **For Service Environment:**
- Create atmosphere matching emotional needs
- Adjust lighting, music, and ambiance accordingly
- Provide appropriate privacy levels
- Ensure emotional safety and comfort

### **For Staff Training:**
- Train staff to recognize emotional cues
- Develop emotional intelligence skills
- Practice empathetic communication
- Learn to adapt service delivery to emotional needs

### **For Business Operations:**
- Schedule appropriate time for emotional support needs
- Create policies supporting emotional well-being
- Train staff in emotional awareness
- Develop emotional support protocols

**These emotional context tags enable more empathetic, personalized service while maintaining simple tag-based organization.**
