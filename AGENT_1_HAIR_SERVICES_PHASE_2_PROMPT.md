### 💡 **Advanced Hair Services Tagging Hints**

#### **Chemical Services (Perm, Keratin)**
- Usually `advanced-expertise` or `intermediate-level`
- Always `extended-session` (2+ hours)
- Typically `premium-service` pricing
- Always `consultation-required` and `moderate-risk` or `higher-risk`
- `long-lasting` results with `high-maintenance` or `moderate-upkeep`

#### **Color Services (Color, Organic Color, Toner)**
- `intermediate-level` to `advanced-expertise`
- `extended-session` for full color, `standard-duration` for toner
- `consultation-required` due to chemical processes
- `moderate-risk` due to potential allergic reactions
- `enhances-color-services` compatibility

#### **Highlighting Services (Highlights, Balayage, Ombre)**
- `intermediate-level` to `advanced-expertise`
- `extended-session` (2-4 hours)
- `premium-service` pricing
- `advanced-technique` complexity
- `transformation-oriented` emotional context
- `special-occasion` and `everyday-appropriate` suitability
