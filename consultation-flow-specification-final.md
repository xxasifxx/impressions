# Consultation Flow System Specification

The consultation flow system efficiently processes high user volumes (100,000+ users) through a streamlined questionnaire with 3-5 targeted questions that rapidly classify users into one of twelve distinct profile types. Based on this classification, the system generates a structured data object that identifies three tiers of service recommendations: primary services (directly satisfying the user's immediate requirements), secondary services (matching their broader interests but not immediate focus), and tertiary services (generally relevant but lower priority). This structured data is passed as props to the landing page builder, which dynamically constructs a personalized experience that prominently features primary services at the top, followed by secondary services in the middle sections, and tertiary services toward the bottom of the page. The implementation emphasizes performance optimization with sub-500ms response times even under heavy load, using a weighted scoring algorithm that accurately maps question responses to the appropriate profile and service tiers without requiring complex branching logic. This approach ensures users quickly discover the most relevant services while still being exposed to the full range of offerings that might interest them based on their profile classification.
