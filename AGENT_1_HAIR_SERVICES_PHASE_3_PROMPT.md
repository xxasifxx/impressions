### 💡 **Specialized Hair Services Tagging Hints**

#### **Color Correction**
- `advanced-expertise` - most complex hair service
- `extended-session` - can take 4-6 hours
- `premium-service` - highest pricing tier
- `higher-risk` - potential for hair damage
- `consultation-required` - must assess current condition
- `transformation-oriented` - dramatic change potential

#### **Hair Extensions**
- `intermediate-level` to `advanced-expertise`
- `extended-session` - 2-4 hours application
- `premium-service` - expensive service and materials
- `long-lasting` - 3-6 months with proper care
- `high-maintenance` - regular upkeep required

#### **Chemical Straightening**
- `advanced-expertise` - complex chemical process
- `extended-session` - 3-4 hours
- `higher-risk` - potential chemical damage
- `consultation-required` - hair assessment needed
- `long-lasting` - permanent until new growth

#### **Consultation Services**
- `beginner-friendly` - accessible to all clients
- `quick-service` - 15-30 minutes
- `budget-friendly` or `mid-range` - planning service
- `confidence-building` - educational and reassuring
- `universal-suitable` - appropriate for everyone

#### **Bridal Services**
- `special-occasion` - wedding-specific
- `celebration-ready` - joyful context
- `premium-service` - special event pricing
- `time-sensitive` - specific date requirements

#### **Express Services**
- `quick-service` - time-efficient
- `budget-friendly` - accessible pricing
- `beginner-friendly` - low complexity
- `flexible-timing` - easy to schedule
