## 🎯 **ENSEMBLE PRICING STRATEGY**

### **Bundle Pricing Logic**
- **Individual Items**: Full retail price
- **Service + Ensemble**: 15-20% discount on accessories
- **Complete Ensemble Packages**: 25-30% discount
- **Maintenance Kits**: 20% discount when purchased with service

### **Consultation Integration**
When customers book services, the consultation should ask:
- "Would you like to maintain these results at home?"
- "Do you have the tools needed for touch-ups?"
- "What's your comfort level with DIY maintenance?"
- "Are you preparing for multiple events?"

## 🎪 **DECISION TREE IMPLICATIONS**

### **Enhanced Consultation Questions**
- "How important is maintaining your look between appointments?"
- "Do you prefer DIY touch-ups or professional maintenance?"
- "What's your comfort level with styling tools?"
- "Are you preparing for one event or multiple occasions?"
- "How much time do you typically spend on daily beauty routines?"

### **Intelligent Bundling Opportunities**
- **Service + Immediate Needs** (bobby pins with updo)
- **Service + Maintenance** (color-safe products with coloring)
- **Service + Comfort** (soothing gel with waxing)
- **Service + Confidence** (touch-up kit with special event makeup)

### **Cosmetologist Logic Patterns**
- "For your bridal updo, you'll want these bobby pins to secure any loose pieces throughout the day"
- "Since you're getting balayage, these color-safe products will help your investment last twice as long"
- "With your sensitive skin facial, this gentle SPF will protect your fresh results"
- "For your makeup lesson, these brushes will let you practice everything we covered today"

**This ensemble approach transforms the consultation from "what service do you want?" to "what complete experience do you need?" - exactly how a considerate cosmetologist would think about customer care.**
