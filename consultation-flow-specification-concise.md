# Consultation Flow Specification

The consultation flow guides users through a personalized discovery process that maps their preferences to appropriate beauty services while evolving the interface aesthetics to match their emerging profile. Beginning with broad questions about desired outcomes (subtle enhancement vs. dramatic transformation, technical precision vs. luxurious experience, or quick refreshment), the system identifies which core user profile (Natural Minimalist, Dramatic Transformer, Precision Specialist, Luxury Indulger, or Quick Refresher) best matches the user. As users respond to increasingly specific questions about their preferences, concerns, and practical constraints, the interface subtly transforms—shifting colors, typography, spacing, and visual elements to align with their profile—creating a sense of progressive personalization that culminates in tailored service recommendations. This approach eliminates the need for users to understand domain-specific terminology or service categories, instead allowing them to express their desired outcomes and aesthetic preferences while the system intelligently maps these to appropriate services across all domains.
